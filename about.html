| BIO |
American artist Sage Delio was born in 1985 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Sage started painting at the age of 4, with her first solo exhibition by age 11. Her degree in Fashion Design helped to hone her skills in painting portraits and figures. A second degree in Computer Science sought to bring her artistic endeavors to new avenues, making video games and experimental videos.

Sage works mainly in the medium of inks and seeks to redefine what it means to be a new media artist, mixing traditional painting with computer simulated watercolors and acrylics. Her work often focuses on cultural ideals of beauty and the complexity of semantics in language. She says, “I tend to take the point of view of an observer, with subjects being what I see or dream. I am just a conduit.”
